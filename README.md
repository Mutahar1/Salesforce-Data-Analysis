A data analysis project focused on exploring and understanding Salesforce revenue and opportunity data using Microsoft Excel. This project delivers actionable insights to improve business performance, optimize revenue channels, and monitor team performance across regions.

---

## Overview

This project aims to provide a clear understanding of Salesforce data through effective cleaning, visualization, and dashboard creation. It helps stakeholders discover key revenue drivers, regional trends, and opportunity sources while addressing data quality challenges.

---

## Problem Statement

Salesforce datasets often include complex, voluminous data that requires careful analysis to reveal actionable insights. Key challenges addressed include:

- Handling missing values and outliers that affect data quality.  
- Identifying the most profitable regions and teams.  
- Analyzing which opportunity sources contribute the most revenue.  
- Creating clear and insightful visualizations for effective communication.  

---

## Tools Used

![Excel](https://img.shields.io/badge/Microsoft_Excel-217346?style=for-the-badge&logo=microsoft-excel&logoColor=white)  
Microsoft Excel was used extensively for data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and dashboard development.

---

## Solutions & Approach

1. **Data Cleaning & Preparation**  
   - Assessed missing values and managed outliers impacting revenue and team data.

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis**  
   - Created heat maps, bar charts, and other visuals to identify trends and revenue patterns.

3. **Dashboard Creation**  
   - Developed interactive Excel dashboards highlighting revenue by country, opportunity sources, and team performance metrics.

---

## Key Findings

- France emerged as one of the top-performing revenue regions.  
- Website and Paid Social are leading opportunity sources driving revenue.  
- The FR & Benelux teams consistently outperform others.  
- Historical revenue trends provide insights into business growth trajectories.

---

## Dashboard Highlights

- **World Revenue Map:** Visualizes revenue distribution by geography.  
- **Revenue by Country Bar Chart:** Shows comparative revenue metrics across countries.  
- **Closed Orders & Opportunity Sources:** Breakdowns by team and channel.

*(Refer to the `images/` folder for screenshots and visuals.)*

---

## Conclusion

This analysis empowers decision-makers to:

- Focus investment on high-impact channels like Website and Paid Social.  
- Leverage strong regional teams such as France and LATAM.  
- Maintain ongoing data quality checks for reliable insights.
